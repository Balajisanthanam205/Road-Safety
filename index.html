About PSW


Revolutionizing Road Safety with Advanced Driver Health Monitoring
Pulse Saver is an innovative solution designed to enhance road safety by continuously monitoring the health of drivers. Our system leverages cutting-edge IoT technology to prevent accidents and provide prompt emergency responses.
Features:

Real-Time Health Monitoring:


Our system tracks vital health parameters like heart rate and pulse, ensuring drivers are in optimal condition.

Collision Prevention:


Advanced algorithms detect signs of driver fatigue or distress, alerting them in real-time to prevent potential accidents.

Emergency Response:


In case of an emergency, Pulse Saver instantly transmits crucial health data to nearby hospitals, enabling quick medical intervention.

User-Friendly Interface:


Our intuitive dashboard allows for easy monitoring and management, providing drivers and fleet operators with actionable insights.

Why Pulse Saver?


Every year, thousands of accidents are caused by health-related issues behind the wheel.
Pulse Saver aims to reduce these incidents by providing a proactive health monitoring system. Our solution not only safeguards drivers but also contributes to a safer driving environment for everyone.
